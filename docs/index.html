The Camp Arc

Five sessions. One arc.

CC Fest Coding Camp runs five sessions over five weeks. The sequence moves from first marks to open studio — each session builds on the last, but every tool and sketch also stands alone. Jump in anywhere.

1 · Your Canvas, Your VoiceShapes, color, coordinates, identity
2 · Things That Move, Things That ListenVariables, animation, events, map(), lerp()
3 · Patterns, Systems, and What They SayLoops, noise, randomness, generative systems
4 · Data as MaterialArrays, mapping, visualization, data storytelling
5 · Open StudioRemix, share, reflect, teach
