Tools for Creative Coding.

Hi, I'm Saber Khan. I run CC Fest Coding Camp, a free five-week program where educators, artists, students, and others learn p5.js and creative coding together. I built these tools for our sessions. 31 workshop tools and 26 starter sketches. They're open. Use them, remix them, make them yours. If you find them useful please get in touch.

How these tools were made.

These tools and sketches were made thanks to the help of LLMs, including Claude and ChatGPT, testing with my students and classes, and learning from members of the community, including Dan Shiffman, Lauren Lee McCarthy, Casey Reas, Kevin Workman, Patt Vira, Allison Parrish, Jeff Thompson, Tim Rodenbröker, p5.js and Processing Foundation materials, and the many people that have shared their teaching materials.

The Camp Arc

Five sessions. One arc.

CC Fest Coding Camp runs five sessions over five weeks. The sequence moves from first marks to open studio. Each session builds on the last, but every tool and sketch also stands alone. Jump in anywhere.

1 · Your Canvas, Your VoiceShapes, color, coordinates, identity
2 · Things That Move, Things That ListenVariables, animation, events, map(), lerp()
3 · Patterns, Systems, and What They SayLoops, noise, randomness, generative systems
4 · Data as MaterialArrays, mapping, visualization, data storytelling
5 · Open StudioRemix, share, reflect, teach
