RGB / HSB Color Lab

Compare two ways of thinking about color in p5.js. RGB mixes red, green, and blue light. HSB lets you work with hue, saturation, and brightness — often a more intuitive system for palettes, moods, gradients, and generative art.

Palette + Actions

RGB asks: how much red, green, and blue light? HSB asks: what color family, how intense, and how bright?

Try this

Make a bright red in RGB, then switch to HSB and change only the hue to create a full color family.

Notice this

In HSB, changing hue can keep a similar brightness and saturation. That makes palettes easier to design.

Remix this

Create a palette for a mood: calm, electric, underwater, sunset, forest, glitch, candy, or storm.
