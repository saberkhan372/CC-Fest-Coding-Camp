dist() measures between two points

dist(x1, y1, x2, y2) returns the distance between two positions. In interactive sketches, that often means “how close is the mouse to something?”

let d = dist(mouseX, mouseY, targetX, targetY);

Big idea

dist() turns nearness into a number.

map() can turn that distance number into size, color, opacity, or motion.

Collision happens when distance is smaller than two radii combined.
